About Us

Our Mission

A 2023 Michigan State University study revealed that across 27,000 students, every one in six had a dietary restriction. In 2018, it was one in eight. Whether it be a religious or life-threatening issue, its becoming clear that there is a prevalent need for an accessible, safer way for students to reliably get the food they deserve.

At UPlate, our mission is to empower students. And it doesn't have to stop at dietary restrictions.

Bottom line is, we believe that students deserve to take control of their nutrition.

Leading nutrition apps are finnicky when it comes to estimating the macros of obscure dining hall specific meals, and they often require tedious manual input. With UPlate, however, you can feel confident knowing you're getting reliable, accurate nutrition about the food served to you, without the guessing and checking.

UPlate is your one-stop meal prep solution for anyone looking to take control of their nutrition.

Our Team

We are a team of 3 freshman CS students from Purdue University, hoping to eventually make a positive impact on college campuses across the nation. UPlate started as an idea at a hackathon, but we soon realized that this was a problem worth solving. Since then, we've worked tirelessly to bring UPlate to life, and we couldn't be more excited to share it with you.
